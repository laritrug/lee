KETO-licious Lobster & Shrimp

Description

Ingredients 3-4 servings 4 4-5oz. Lobster Tails 15 Jumbo Shrimp Asparagus 1 Large Lemon 2 tbsp. Dried Italian Parsley 1 tbsp. Curry Powder 2 tsp. Garlic Powder 1 1/2 sticks Butter Pink Himalayan Salt Ground Fresh Pepper

Cooking

1

Thaw Lobsters and Shrimp in an ice bath. Then shell both. Save Lobster shells for presentation. Set oven to Broil. In a baking pan, line with Asparagus and season with Olive Oil, Salt & Pepper. Put in oven

2

In one bowl add Shrimp, Curry Powder, Dried Italian Parsely, 2 tabs of melted butter. In a seperate dish, add diced Lobster meat, Dried Italian Parsely, 2 tabs of melted butter, Garlic Powder.

3

Take out Asparagus and line shells on top. Fill shells with Lobster meat, and drizzle remaining butter generously. Lay shrimp in between shells. Broil for 8-10 min. Garnish with Salt, Pepper and Lemon juice over all.
